  • Fragrant house plants are a great way to spruce up your space and decor with some pleasant aromas and eye-catching appeal.
  • Studies have proven that house plants improve concentration and productivity (by up to 15%), reduce stress levels, and boost your mood.
  • The same dust that settles on your computer screen also settles on your plants and inhibits the leaves from properly photosynthesizing. Dusting houseplants can be accomplished with a damp cloth and some patience
